# Uber Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## Overview
This project presents a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Uber’s business performance using simulated data. The dashboard provides insights into booking trends, revenue patterns, vehicle performance, customer behavior, payment preferences, and location-based demand.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate an end-to-end data analytics workflow, from understanding business requirements to delivering insights through interactive visualizations.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze overall booking and revenue performance  
- Identify demand patterns and booking losses  
- Understand vehicle-wise and payment-wise revenue contribution  
- Support data-driven business decision-making  

---

## Key KPIs
- **Completed Bookings:** 93K  
- **Lost Bookings:** 57K  
- **Total Revenue:** 52M  
- **Total Distance Covered:** 3M km  
- **Average Trip Distance:** 24.6 km  

**Insight:**  
Lost bookings form a significant share of total demand, indicating unmet supply during peak periods.

---

## Dashboard Analysis

### Booking Performance
- Monthly and quarterly trends show variations in completed bookings and revenue
- Certain periods experience high demand pressure, while others indicate scope for promotions

### Vehicle Type Analysis
**Vehicle Categories Analyzed:**
- Auto  
- Bike  
- Go Mini  
- Go Sedan  
- Premier Sedan  
- Uber XL  

**Insights:**
- Economy vehicles generate high booking volumes
- Premium vehicles have lower ride counts but higher revenue per trip

### Revenue Analysis
- Revenue breakdown by vehicle type, customer, and payment method
- Seasonal revenue trends observed
- A small group of customers contributes a higher share of revenue

### Payment Method Analysis
**Payment Methods:**
- UPI  
- Cash  
- Uber Wallet  
- Credit Card  
- Debit Card  

**Insights:**
- UPI is the most commonly used payment method
- Digital payments dominate completed bookings

### Location Analysis
- Certain pickup and drop locations act as consistent demand hotspots
- Useful for driver allocation and surge pricing strategies

### Ratings Analysis
- **Average Customer Rating:** 4.40  
- **Average Driver Rating:** 4.23  

Overall experience is positive, with slightly lower driver ratings during peak hours.

---

## Key Findings
- High lost bookings indicate unmet demand
- Economy vehicles drive ride volume
- Premium vehicles drive higher revenue per ride
- Digital payments dominate transactions
- Revenue shows seasonal variation

---

## Business Recommendations
- Improve driver availability during peak hours
- Reduce lost bookings through demand–supply optimization
- Promote premium vehicles with targeted incentives
- Encourage digital payments for smoother operations
- Use historical trends for demand forecasting
